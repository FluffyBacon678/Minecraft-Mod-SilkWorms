## Description body

### 🐛 → 🥚 → 🦋 A tiny life cycle, done gently

**Silkworms** adds one small, complete story to your world: little silkworms
graze grass *vegetation* (never grass blocks — your builds are safe), spin
hanging cocoons under leaves, and hatch into elegant cream-and-tan silk
moths with layered eye-spot wings.

Find them in **forests**, or discover little colonies living on the moss in
**lush caves**.

### 🎨 Eight natural colour morphs

Silkworms come in eight soft, natural colours — Cream, Mulberry Green, Mossy
Silk, Ash Gray, Rust Brown, Golden Silk, Cherry Blossom and Snow White — and
cocoons inherit their worm's tint. Carry up to **5 worms in one bucket**; it
remembers each worm's colour.

### 🦋 A companion that keeps up

Tame a silk moth with **cherry leaves**. Tamed moths grow to twice the size,
wear a little harness, never die of old age, and follow you *properly* —
including catch-up flight and safe mid-air relocation when you elytra away.
They defend you like a wolf (hostile mobs only, never creepers).

### 🕹️ And yes — you can fly it

Right-click your tamed moth with an empty hand and take off. Flight follows
your look (aimed gently above it, so glancing at the ground doesn't dive),
jump climbs, idle hovers, sneak dismounts safely. Faster than a Happy Ghast;
calmer than elytra.

### 🧹 Honest uninstall tooling

Silkworms ships its own safe-removal commands: a dry-run counter and a
cleanup that converts tamed moths into named vanilla Allays and removes
everything else — so you can uninstall without corrupting anything and
without silently losing your pet. Full honesty notes in the repo's
`COMPATIBILITY_AND_REMOVAL.md`.

### 🧩 Modpack-friendly by design

No mixins, no access wideners, no custom networking, no blocks or world
data. Verified in a 119-mod Fabric pack (Sodium/Iris/Lithium and friends).
