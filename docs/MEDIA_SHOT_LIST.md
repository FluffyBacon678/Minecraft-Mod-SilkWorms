# Silkworms — Screenshot / Video Shot List

For the Modrinth gallery + a ~60s trailer. Shoot at high render distance,
fancy graphics, daylight unless noted. F1 to hide the HUD.

## Screenshots (gallery order)

1. **Hero shot** — refined moth hovering over a flowering azalea or cherry
   grove, camera slightly below, wings mid-flap. Golden-hour light.
2. **Lifecycle triptych** (three shots, same grassy scene):
   a. silkworm grazing short grass, b. cocoon hanging under oak leaves,
   c. moth just after hatch (poof particles if timed well).
3. **Riding** — third-person (F5) over a forest canopy, moth + rider centred,
   horizon visible. Bonus: a second tamed moth following in frame.
4. **Lush-cave discovery** — a 3–4 worm colony on moss beside glow berries,
   player torch-lit in the background.
5. **Colour morphs line-up** — 8 worms on a moss platform, one per variant,
   bucket in hand (tooltip open showing "Silkworms: 5/5" + variants line).
6. **Tamed vs wild** — 2× harnessed moth beside a wild moth for the size
   difference; both facing camera (cute faces visible).
7. **Config screen** — Mod Menu → Silkworms settings, for the tech-minded.

## Trailer beats (~60 seconds)

| t | Beat |
|---|---|
| 0–8s | Lush cave reveal: torchlight over moss, worms come into view |
| 8–18s | Bucket scoop (tooltip visible), carry home, release in a garden |

| 36–46s | Taming: cherry leaves ×3, hearts, harness pops in, moth grows |
| 46–58s | Flight sequence: takeoff, canopy skim, gentle dive, safe dismount |

## Capture tips

- The pitch-offset flight looks best in third person while gently weaving.
- For the hatch shot: set cocoon growth to ~15s in the config first.
- Spawn variant worms fast: bucket in creative keeps rolled colours.
- Shaders (Iris) are verified-compatible — one shader shot is fine, but keep
  at least the lifecycle shots vanilla so the store page matches defaults.
